## Applied Research at FBK

### About FBK
Fondazione Bruno Kessler is a private entity established in 1962 by the Autonomous Province of Trento. The mission of the foundation is to promote and contribute to the advancement of knowledge, with a focus on the fields of science and technology that allow for greater and more immediate economic and social benefits. The bond with the territory is not only administrative, but it reflects a deeply embedded vocation to serve as an engine of knowledge for the region and, by extension, for the broader European research landscape.

FBK operates as a scientific and technological hub, its premises and platforms hosting a lively ecosystem of co-located ventures, spin-offs, projects and training opportunities. The campus spans two hubs: one dedicated to technologies and innovation, located at Povo on the hills of Trento, the other dedicated to the humanities and social sciences, in the heart of the city.

The strength soul FBK draws substantially from the diversity and density of its human resources. The foundation hosts a significant presence of foreign researchers, alongside doctoral students, visiting professors and affiliated scholars from across Europe and beyond. This international composition generates a productive cross-contamination of methodologies, backgrounds and perspectives, a distinctive trait that shapes the quality and ambition of the research conducted here.
